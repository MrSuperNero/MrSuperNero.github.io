Infrastructure

Internet infrastructure: The connection between the digital and physical worlds.

These are Internet cables running through the streets

No one was present. All was quiet.

And yet, the street was packed and crowded, loud with presence and connection.

It is interesting to see how well-integrated the Internet is into our lives. Even though it is a digital, fabricated realm, its influence spills over into our world. We can touch the Internet and see its connections.
